INVESTMENT CALCULATOR

GOAL: A simple investment calculator that takes an initial investment amount in dollars. Then accepts an interest rate between 2-7%. Then takes an additional amount of money for each term of the investment. Enter a investment term(ie. how long). Then specify that the term will be in years or months. Result will be your total interest earned and the total amount in your account.

  1. START
  2. DISPLAY "What's your initial investment amount?"
  3. INPUT initialInvestment
  4. DISPLAY "What is your interest rate? (must be 2 through 7)"
  5. INPUT interestRate
  6. If interestRate is 2, (afterInterestRate = initialInvestment / .02)
  7. If interestRate is 3, (afterInterestRate = initialInvestment / .03)
  8. If interestRate is 4, (afterInterestRate =initialInvestment / .04)
  9. If interestRate is 5, (afterInterestRate = initialInvestment / .05)
  10. If interestRate is 6, (afterInterestRate = initialInvestment / .06)
  11. If interestRate is 7, (afterInterestRate = initialInvestment / .07)
  12. DISPLAY afterInterestRate
  13. DISPLAY "Any additional?"
  14. INPUT additionalTerm
  15. afterAdditionalTerm = afterInterestRate - additionalTerm
  16. DISPLAY "Ok, how long will the investment term be?"
  17. Give a option for months
    1. If answer is in months (afterAdditionalTerm
  18. Give a option for years
